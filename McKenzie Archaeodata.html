About

I am a Master’s Candidate at the University of Victoria defending my thesis in November 2024. My research focuses on broadening the scope and accessibility of the University of Victoria Zooarchaeological Lab (UVicZL) facility’s comparative collection. I'm combining applied library science and open data to “extend” collection specimens with biodiversity and taxonomic information, Indigenous language areas, ontological terminology, biometric, and geospatial location data. My research will enhance access, improve discoverability, and expand UVicZL collection data as a citable reference resource. UVicZL is an essential resource used to expand deep time appreciation of how Indigenous peoples sustained themselves and managed their coastal landscapes for millennia. My goal is to improve access, link Indigenous knowledge, expand outreach education, and foster ecological and ethnobiological research.
